Ayam Gepuk Recipe

Ingredients

Ayam Gepuk :

  • 1/2 Chicken
  • 4 Small red onion cloves
  • 1 Piece live turmeric
  • 3 Cloves garlic
  • 1 1/2 Tablespoons coriander seeds
  • Water (for grinding)
  • Salt
  • 2 PIeces bay leaves

    • Sambal Gepuk :

  • 12-15 Stalks of chili
  • 3 Cloves garlic
  • 2-3 Tablespoons peanuts
  • 2 Teaspoons sugar
  • Salt
  • Seasoning powder (optional)
  • Hot oil

    • Side Ingredients :

  • Excess chicken marinade
  • 1 tablespoon wheat flour
  • 2 Tablespoons rice flour
  • 4 Pieces tofu
  • 2 Pieces tempe
  • Cooking oil

    • Other Ingredients :

  • Cabbage
  • Tofu
  • Cucumber
  • Lime

    • Preparation Method

    Ayam Gepuk :

    1. Finely grind small shallots, live turmeric, garlic, coriander seeds with a little water.
    2. Mix the shredded chicken with a little salt and finely ground ingredients and marinate for a while or overnight.
    3. After marinating, boil the chicken with a little water and add bay leaves. Boil until soft and cooked.
    4. Remove and toss the boiled chicken.
    5. Fry the chicken for a while until browned and just crisp on the outside of the chicken.
    6. Mash the chicken until it crumbles in a bowl of sambal rub.

    Sambal Gepuk :

    1. Fry the peanuts then remove and drain the oil.
    2. Fry the rice chili and garlic briefly for about 3-4 minutes until wilted.
    3. Finely mash peanuts with sugar and remove.
    4. Finely mash fried chili rice and garlic and a little salt and seasoning powder (optional).
    5. Add hot frying oil and peanut paste to the sambal.
    6. Add a squeeze of lime juice if desired.

    Frying :

    1. Cut tofu and tempeh.
    2. Add wheat flour and rice flour to the excess boiled water as a mixture coating
    3. Marinate the tofu and tempeh for a while.
    4. Fry the cabbage pieces until slightly brown (optional).
    5. Fry the tofu marinade and tempeh dip until browned and crisp.

    Serving :

    1. Serve with white rice, gepuk chicken, a little sambal on top of the chicken with tempeh tofu, musk lime, cucumber and cabbage.
    2. Then, Pak Gembus-style gepuk chicken is ready to be served and enjoyed.
